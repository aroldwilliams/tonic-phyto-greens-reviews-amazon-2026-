# Antioxidants: Why Are They Important?

One of the most common terms you'll see in supplement descriptions is **"antioxidants."**

But what exactly are they?

During everyday life, your body's normal metabolic processes produce unstable molecules known as **free radicals**.

These molecules are a natural part of life.

However, when free radicals build up faster than the body's natural defenses can manage them, they contribute to **oxidative stress**.

Many fruits, vegetables, herbs, and colorful plant foods naturally contain antioxidant compounds.

These antioxidants help protect cells from oxidative stress and are one reason health experts recommend eating a wide variety of plant-based foods.

A greens powder provides another convenient way to increase your intake of these naturally occurring compounds.
